PROC‑AIA‑005 · Vendor AI Addendum

Attach to solicitations and contracts for HR software with AI features.

Required clauses (copy into solicitation/contract)

  1. AI Feature Description – Vendor shall disclose all AI/ML features, intended uses, model types, and where in the workflow AI is invoked.
  2. Data Use & Retention – University data shall not be used for model training outside the UMN tenant. Retention and deletion schedules shall be specified.
  3. Bias & Fairness Testing – Vendor shall provide documented pre‑deployment bias testing, ongoing monitoring methods, and material results relevant to HR use.
  4. Explainability & Documentation – Vendor shall furnish end‑user‑readable explanations and technical documentation sufficient for audit.
  5. Audit & Logs – UMN shall have audit rights and access to activity logs relating to AI features (inputs, outputs, model versions) subject to security controls.
  6. Subprocessors – Vendor shall disclose all subprocessors with AI access and notify UMN of changes in advance.
  7. Incident Response – AI‑related security/privacy incidents must be reported within contractually specified timelines and include corrective actions.
  8. Configuration & Controls – AI features shall be configurable to disable or constrain as needed; defaults shall align with UMN policy.
  9. Termination & Data Return/Deletion – Upon termination, return and then delete University data, certifying deletion.

Optional due‑diligence questionnaire

  • Has the vendor completed an algorithmic impact assessment for HR uses?
  • Can the vendor provide representative test cases and known limitations?
  • Is there a documented human‑in‑the‑loop configuration for high‑stakes actions?
  • Are there documented controls to prevent model drift or feature changes without notice?

TA‑001 · JD & Job Ad Prompt Guide

Use with Public data only. Keep human review. Attach this to the requisition packet.

Prompts (copy/paste)

You are an HR Talent Acquisition specialist at a public research university.
Task: Improve this job ad to be accurate, inclusive, and scannable.
Constraints:
- Preserve minimum and preferred qualifications verbatim.
- Remove jargon and potentially biased terms.
- Add 2–3 concise impact statements tied to the department’s mission.
- Keep length under 150 words.
Input:
- Job Title:
- Unit/Dept:
- Minimum Qualifications (verbatim):
- Preferred Qualifications (verbatim):
- Key Duties (bullets):
Output:
- 3 ad variants (General / Research-focused / Student-facing)
- Inclusive alternatives for any flagged terms
- A 120-character snippet for social posting

Reviewer checklist (attach)

  • Minimum & preferred qualifications preserved verbatim.
  • Salary range and union/contract language validated (if applicable).
  • Inclusive language check completed (remove gendered idioms, ageist terms, etc.).
  • EEO/affirmative action statement included.
  • Human-in-the-loop reviewer signed off (see HIL‑003).
